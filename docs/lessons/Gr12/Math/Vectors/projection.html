Scalar & Vector Projections

Scalar Projection

of \(\vec{a}\text{ on }\vec{b}=\vert\text{proj}\left(\vec{a}\text{ on }\vec{b}\right)\vert=\vert\vec{a}\vert\cos{θ} \)

=\(\dfrac{\vec{a}\cdot\vec{b}}{\vert\vec{b}\vert} \)

of on \(\vec{b}\text{ on }\vec{a}=\vert\text{proj}\left(\vec{b}\text{ on }\vec{a}\right)\vert=\dfrac{\vec{a}\cdot\vec{b}}{\vert\vec{a}\vert} \)

Vector Projection of \(\vec{b}\text{ on }\vec{a} \)

in the direction of \(\vec{b} \)

proj\(\left(\vec{a}\text{ on }\vec{b}\right)=\dfrac{\vec{a}\cdot\vec{b}}{\vert\vec{b}\vert} \hat{b} \)

\(=\left(\dfrac{\vec{a}\cdot\vec{b}}{\vec{b}\cdot\vec{b}}\right)\vec{b}=\dfrac{\vec{a}\cdot\vec{b}}{\vert\vec{b}\vert^2}\vec{b} \)

Find teh scalar and vector projections of \(\vec{u}\text{ onto }\vec{v}\text{, if }\vec{u}=(5, 6, -3)\text{ and }\vec{v}=(1, 4, 5) \)

\(\vert\text{proj}\left(\vec{u}\cdot\text{ on }\vec{v}\right)\vert=\dfrac{\vec{u}\cdot\vec{v}}{\vert\vec{v}\vert}´=\dfrac{5(1)+6(4)+-3(5)}{\sqrt{1^2+4^2+5^2}}=\dfrac{14}{\sqrt{42}}×\dfrac{\sqrt{42}}{42}=\dfrac{14\sqrt{42}}{42}=\dfrac{1}{8}\sqrt{42} \)

proj\(\left(\vec{u}\text{ on }\vec{v}\right)=\dfrac{\vec{u}\cdot\vec{v}}{\vec{v}\cdot\vec{v}}=\dfrac{14}{42}(1, 4, 5)=\left(\dfrac{1}{3}, \dfrac{4}{3}, \dfrac{5}{3}\right) \)

Find teh scalar and vector projections of \(\vec{v}\text{ onto }\vec{u} \)

\(\vert\text{proj}\left(\vec{v}\text{ on }\vec{u}\right)\vert=\dfrac{\vec\cdot\vec{u}}{\vert\vec{u}\vert}=\dfrac{1(5)+9(6)+(-3)(5)}{\sqrt{5^2+6^2+3^2}} \)

\(=\dfrac{14}{\sqrt{70}} \)

proj \(\left(\vec{v}\text{ on }\vec{u}\right)=\dfrac{\left(\vec{v}\cdot\vec{u}\right)}{\left(\vec{u}\cdot\vec{u}\right)\vec{u} } \)

\(=\dfrac{14}{70}(5, 6, -3) \)

\(=\dfrac{1}{5}(5, 6, -3) \)
