3. Jake and Maria are towing their freinds on toboggan. Jake is exerting a force of 65N and Maria a force of 60N. Since they are walking side by side, the ropes pull to eitehr side of the toboggan at 40° to each other.

a. Find the resultant force pulling the toboggan forward from a stop.

\(\vert\vec{f_{R}}\vert^2=65^2+60^2-2(65)(60)cos 140° \)

\(\vert\vec{f_{R}}\vert=117.5N\)

\( \dfrac{sinθ}{65}=\dfrac{sin140°}{117.5} \)

\( θ=21° \)

\(\vec{f_{R}}=117.5N [\text{21° off of 60N force}] \)

b. Soon the toboggan is travelling at a constant speed. Find the equilibrant force and explain what it represents.

\(\vec{f_{E}}=117.5N [\text{159° off of 60N force}] \)
