Dot Product (Geometric)

Find the dot product for \(\vec{u}\cdot\vec{v} \) for each of the following where θ is the angle between vectors.

a. \(\vert\vec{u}\vert=7.\vert\vec{v}\vert=12.θ=60° \)

b. \(\vert\vec{a}\vert=20,\vert\vec{b}\vert=3,θ=\dfrac{5\pi}{6} \)

a. \(\vec{u}\cdot\vec{v}=(7)(12)\cos{60°}=42 \)

b. \(\text{try yourself leave exact answer} -30\sqrt{3} \)

For the above question a. find \(\vec{u}\cdot\vec{v} \). What is property you can conclude from this

\(\vec{v}\cdot\vec{u}=(7)(12)\cos{60°}=42 \)

∴\(\vec{v}\cdot\vec{u}=\vec{v}\cdot\vec{u} \)

ie. dot product is COMMUTATIVE

\(\vec{a}\cdot\vec{a}=(20)(20)\cos{0°}=400 \)

\(\vec{b}\cdot\vec{b}=9 \)

ie.

\(\vec{a}\cdot\vec{a}=\vert\vec{a}\vert^2 \)

Dot product Properties

\(\vec{u}\cdot\vec{u}=0 \) iff \(\vec{u}\perp\vec{v} \)

\(\vec{u}\cdot\vec{v}=\vec{v}\cdot\vec{u} \)

\(\vec{u}\cdot\vec{u}=\vert\vec{u}\vert^2 \)

\(\vec{u}\cdot\vec{0}=0 (scalar) \)

\(\vec{u}\cdot\left(\vec{a}+\vec{b}\right)=\vec{u}\cdot\vec{a}+\vec{u}\cdot\vec{b} \)

\(k\left(\vec{u}\cdot\vec{v}\right)=\left(\vec{ku}\right)\cdot\vec{v}=\vec{u}\cdot\left(\vec{ku}\right) \)
