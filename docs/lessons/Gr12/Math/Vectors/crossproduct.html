VECTORS

Cross Product

Definition of the geometric cross product of \( \vec{a}\) and \(\vec{b}\):

\( \vec{a}\times\vec{b} = \vert\vec{a}\vert\vert\vec{b}\vert\sin{\theta}\text{ }\hat{n} \)

\( \hat{n} \), is the normal vector perpendicular to \( \vec{a}\) and \(\vec{b}\)

\( \theta \), is the angle between the tails.

Cross Product Properties

1. \( \vec{a}\times\vec{b} = -\vec{b}\times\vec{a} \) not commutative
2. \( \vec{a}\times(\vec{b}+c)=\vec{a}\times\vec{b}+\vec{a}\times\vec{c} \) distributive
3. \( k(\vec{a}\times\vec{b}) = \vec{a}\times k\vec{b} = k\vec{a}\times\vec{b} \)
4. \( (\vec{a}\times\vec{b})\times\vec{c} \ne \vec{a}\times(\vec{b}\times\vec{c}) \) not associative
5. \( \vec{u}\cdot(v\times\vec{w}) = (\vec{u}\times\vec{v})\cdot\vec{w} \) triple scalar product - used to prove coplanar if equals zero
6. \( \vec{a}\times\vec{a} \) = 0

Find \( \vert\vec{u}\times\vec{v}\vert \) for each of the following pairs of vectors. State whether \( \vec{u}\times\vec{v} \) is directed into or out of the page.

\( \vert\vec{u}\times\vec{v}\vert = \vert\vec{u}\vert\vert\vec{v}\vert\sin{\theta} \)

\( = (12)(5)\sin{68°} \)

\( = 55.6 \)

\( \therefore \text{ } \vec{u}\times\vec{v} = 55.6 \text{ units} \) [Into the page]

\( \vert\vec{u}\times\vec{v}\vert = \vert\vec{u}\vert\vert\vec{v}\vert\sin{\theta} \)

\( = (18)(25)\sin{120°} \)

\( = 450\left(\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\right) \)

\( \therefore \text{ } \vec{u}\times\vec{v} = 225 \sqrt{3} \text{ units} \) [Out of the page]

State whether the following expressions are vectors, scalars or meaningless

\( \vec{a}\cdot(\vec{b}\times\vec{c}) = \) scalar
\( \vec{a}\times(\vec{b}\cdot\vec{c}) = \) meaningless
\( \vec{a}\cdot(\vec{b}\cdot\vec{c}) = \) meaningless
\( \vec{a}\times(\vec{b}\times\vec{c}) = \) vector
\( (\vec{a}\cdot\vec{b})\times(\vec{b}\cdot\vec{c}) = \) meaningless
\( (\vec{a}\times\vec{b})\cdot(\vec{b}\times\vec{c}) = \) scalar
\( (\vec{a}\times\vec{b})+(\vec{b}\times\vec{c}) = \) vector
\( (\vec{a}\cdot\vec{b})+(\vec{b}\cdot\vec{c}) = \) scalar
\( (\vec{a}+\vec{b})\cdot\vec{c} = \) scalar
\( (\vec{a}+\vec{b})\times\vec{c} = \) vector
\( (\vec{a}\times\vec{b})-\vec{c} = \) vector
\( (\vec{a}\cdot\vec{b})-\vec{c} = \) meaningless
